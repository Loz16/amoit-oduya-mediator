ABOUT ME

Mediation with
Purpose

I am a community mediator and an advocate with Pokot Women's Health Rights (PWHR) in West Pokot County, Kenya. My work focuses on resolving family, land, and community disputes while advocating for women's and girls' rights, gender equality, and the elimination of harmful cultural practices. I believe in empowering communities to resolve conflicts peacefully and build lasting relationships based on respect, fairness, and justice.
